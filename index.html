Our Mission

Subterranean Activities is not just your ordinary record store. We provide a wide selection of countless genres for our music-loving customers. Our store carries new and used vinyl and cassettes, VHS tapes and DVDs, vintage posters, and band merchandise. You can also partake in the arcade room located in the back. Please visit us during our buying hours if you want to purge any of your records. Nostalgia is what we aim for, come check us out!
